About EvoTech 1.0

EVOTECH is the flagship event of BVEST, annual tech fest of BVCOE, launched with a vision to let the young developers portray their products solving real-world problems and inspire others.

There are three parallel tracks for project submissions - Evolve, Innovate & Amplify. For more event details, eligibility and judging criteria, click here.
The event is held in two stages:
1. Summary and Details of the project (Online) &
2. Exhibition and Competition (Offline)

* Both Software and Hardware projects are allowed.
